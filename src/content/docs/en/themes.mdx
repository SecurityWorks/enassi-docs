We offer many themes, [based on @radix-ui/colors](https://www.radix-ui.com/colors/docs/palette-composition/scales).

By default, it will automatically detect whether it is a dark theme. If it is not detected properly, you can check **Force use of dark theme** in **Settings** -> **Appearance**.
